About Me

A passionate and inquisitive full stack developer based in Sunnyvale, California. I spent most of my life in Honolulu, Hawaii, where I was in the fishing industry and learned a lot of skills like persistance and hard work.

Fuelled by curiosity and innovation, I made the decision to transsition to Sunnyvale, California, to become a developer. I discovered my passion for creating and developing ideas to life, enjoying the process of problem-solving, and bringing solutions to fruition.

In my free time, you can catch me shooting hoops and exploring all the good food the new city I live in has to offer.
